# A Land Remembered — Website Project

## Project Overview

A new website for **A Land Remembered**. The site serves two primary goals:
1. **Drive viewers to the series**
2. **Sell books**

### Existing Sites
- [alandremembered.com](http://alandremembered.com) — books
- [alandremembered.org](http://alandremembered.org) — web series

---

## Three Proposed Directions

### Approach 1 — Minimal Hub (`/approach-1-minimal/`)
**Starting at $1,850**

Focused, clean design that gets out of the way. Points visitors where they need to go: watch the series, buy the books. Emphasis on typography, pacing, and editorial clarity.

**Primary concept:** Design language inspired by the covers, textures, and typography of the book titles. Viewers can sift through different spines and view the trailer. Minimal menu and interactions.

**References:**
- [Operate](https://operate.so/)
- [Museo Thyssen — Volver a la Naturaleza](https://www.museothyssen.org/webdocs/conectathyssen/volver-naturaleza/en/)
- [Ancestry — Stories of Us](https://www.ancestry.com/stories-of-us)
- [Library by Obys](https://library.obys.agency/books/)

---

### Approach 2 — Dynamic & Interactive (`/approach-2-dynamic/`)
**Starting at $2,500**

One step into a dynamic experience. Interactive elements draw visitors in and paint a clearer picture of the world. Still primarily funnels visitors to books and series, but rewards exploration.

**Primary concept:** Situated in a breathing Florida landscape, the reader's scroll reveals details about the series. A log line as they sit in the wild swamp. When they've exhausted the narrative scroll, they can access different books, watch the trailer, and learn about characters. A guided experience.

**References:**
- [Sutera](https://www.sutera.ch/)
- [Air](https://air.inc/)
- [Nildo](https://www.nildo.it/en)

---

### Approach 3 — Experiential (`/approach-3-experiential/`)
**Starting at $6,000**

The site is a destination in itself, becoming a new way to interact with the IP. Environments, narrative-driven navigation, light browser-game elements. The Florida frontier brought to life in the browser.

**Primary concept:** An overview of a Florida swamp, landmarking specific areas with details from different books. Clips and stills from the show featured within panels that open.

**References:**
- [Explore Primland](https://explore.ownprimland.com/)
- [TheVertMenthe by Dault-Lafon](https://thevertmenthe.dault-lafon.fr/)
- [ETDS](https://www.e-t-d-s.com/)
- [National Geographic — Into the Amazon](https://www.nationalgeographic.com/into-the-amazon/)
